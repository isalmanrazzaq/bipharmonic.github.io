Our Services

Bipharmonic offers professional freight dispatch services across the USA. We help owner-operators and small fleets with dry van, reefer, flatbed, and hotshot dispatching. We finds top-paying loads, handles paperwork, and provides 24/7 support — all with no forced dispatch. We also assist with bookkeeping, accounts receivable, and factoring support so you can focus on driving while we manage the back office.

Flat Bed & ​Step Deck Dispatch ​​Services

At BiPharmonic Dispatch, we specialize in flat bed & step deck trailer dispatching, connecting drivers with lucrative and reliable freight opportunities across the nation. We have experience booking overweight and over-dimensional loads. Our truck dispatchers understand the unique challenges and advantages of flat bed & step deck transportation, ensuring you maximize your earning potential. 

Dry Van Dispatch Services

Whether you’re transporting retail goods, manufacturing supplies, or e-commerce shipments, BiPharmonic has the expertise to keep your dry van fully utilized and your business thriving. We handle the complexities of load booking and management, allowing you to focus on what you do best – driving and delivering. Partner with Truck Dispatch 360 and experience how our specialized dry van dispatching can drive your success to new heights.

Reefer Dispatch Services

BiPharmonic excels in providing premium reefer dispatch services, connecting drivers with high-value temperature-controlled freight opportunities across the United States. Our specialized truck dispatchers understand the unique challenges and requirements of reefer transportation, helping you maximize your earnings with our truck dispatch services.
