Our freight dispatchers work with you to develop a lane that will achieve your target goals. Truck Dispatch Operations Include:
  1. Load Sourcing: Continuously searching for available loads that match a carrier’s criteria.
  2. Freight Rate Negotiation: Securing the best possible rates through tough negotiations with brokers.
  3. Carrier Packets: Completing carrier packets in order setup with brokers.
  4. Dispatching the Driver: Sending out the rate confirmation to the driver and dispatching the driver.
  5. Tracking Load Status: Monitoring the progress of shipments and providing updates to all parties.
  6. Invoicing and Payments: Submitting paperwork to factoring companies for invoicing.

#1 – Truck Dispatch Services is more than just finding freight loads

It’s obvious that a truck dispatcher finds loads for carriers, but they also offer other services that can benefit your operation as a whole. 

Motor Carrier Compliance: Our truck dispatchers will ensure that you maintain proper motor carrier compliance so that you can stay out of trouble and continue earning a living.
Customer Care: Our dispatchers will not only help find high-paying loads, but we will also help you manage relationships with shippers, establish service expectations with brokers, and tackle any complex issues that may arise.
Manage Unexpected Delays: As a truck dispatch company, we are responsible for helping you address unexpected delays caused by weather, traffic and other issues while you’re on the road. Dispatch services have incentives to ensure that the entire process from load booking to negotiating and transporting goes as smoothly.

#2 – Outsourcing Dispatching Services Saves Time

As an owner-operator, you may start out booking loads yourself using load boards. This may work well for a while, but once you start accepting loads, you won’t have enough time to spend on load boards. You’re also missing out on opportunities to make more money on higher-paying loads.

That’s where a dispatching company can help. Our dispatcher will give you more time to focus on working on your business and less time searching for loads.

The main cause of failure for most beginner owner-operators is due to a lack of finding consistent loads to keep their business moving. Time is money.

#3 – Truck Dispatchers Work For You

As truck dispatchers providing freight dispatching services, we take a percentage of your rate. This gives us the incentive to negotiate high rates on your behalf. The more money you make, the more money we make.

Got Questions?

What exactly does a truck dispatch service do?

A truck dispatch service acts as a liaison between shippers and carriers, managing load assignments, route planning, paperwork, and communication to ensure efficient freight transportation.

Can dispatch services help me find better-paying loads?

Yes, professional dispatchers, like the team at BiPharmonic Dispatch, have extensive networks and access to load boards, enabling them to secure higher-paying freight opportunities for their clients.

Do I need dispatch services if I’m an owner-operator?

While not mandatory, many owner-operators benefit from dispatch services as they allow more time for driving and less stress from managing business operations.

How do dispatch services handle different trailer types?

Experienced dispatch services are well-versed in the requirements for various trailer types (dry van, flatbed, reefer, etc.) and can match you with appropriate loads and manage specific needs for each.

Will using a dispatch service affect my relationship with my current customers?

A good dispatch service will maintain and even enhance your customer relationships by providing professional communication and reliable service coordination.

How does a dispatch service help with compliance and regulations?        

Dispatch services stay updated on industry regulations and can assist with paperwork, permits, and ensuring your operations meet all legal requirements.

Can I still choose my own routes and loads with a dispatch service?

Yes, most dispatch services including BiPharmonic work collaboratively with drivers, considering preferences while offering expert advice on optimal routes and loads.

How does professional dispatching differ from self-dispatching?

Professional dispatching leverages industry connections, advanced technology, and market expertise to secure better loads and rates, optimize routes, and handle paperwork, allowing drivers to focus on driving.

Can a dispatch service guarantee loads?

While no service can guarantee loads 100% of the time, professional dispatchers significantly increase the likelihood of consistent, high-quality freight opportunities through their extensive networks and market knowledge.

How quickly can I start seeing results with a dispatch service?

Many owner-operators report seeing improvements within the first month, with significant gains in efficiency and profitability often realized within 1-3 months of partnering with a professional dispatch service.

Will using a dispatch service affect my independence as an owner-operator?

No, a good dispatch service enhances your independence by handling time-consuming tasks, allowing you to make final decisions while benefiting from expert advice and support.

Can dispatch services help with back-office tasks like invoicing and collections?

Yes, many dispatch services offer comprehensive back-office support, including invoicing, collections, and even factoring services to improve cash flow.

Is it cost-effective for a small fleet to use a dispatch service?

Yes, many small fleets find that the increased efficiency, better load quality, and reduced administrative burden more than offset the cost of professional dispatching services.

How quickly can I get started with BiPharmonic?

We can typically onboard new clients within 24-48 hours, getting you connected to better loads almost immediately.

What types of trucks and trailers do you support?

We specialize in dispatching for dry van, flatbed, reefer, step deck, and RGN trailers, catering to a wide range of transportation needs.

How does your pricing work?

We offer transparent, percentage-based pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs and volume.
