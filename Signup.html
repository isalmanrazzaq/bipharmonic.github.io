Getting Started With BiPharmonic

Our Truck Dispatch Services setup packet is simple. Once you’re ready to start working with us, we will forward you a link to a carrier application to get started. Once we have all the paperwork, you’ll be introduced to your personal dispatcher and start work immediately. We are an experienced and reliable dispatch company with great relationships among the carriers we work with. That’s why our onboarding is smooth and streamlined.
Here is what you need to sign up:

Signed Truck Dispatch Service Agreement
Factoring Information (if any)
General Power Of Attorney (allows us to contact brokers on your behalf)
Copy Of Your MC Authority Letter
Copy Of The Insurance Certificate
Driver’s Contact Info
Copy Of W-9 Form
